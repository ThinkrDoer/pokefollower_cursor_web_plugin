## Welcome, Trainer! 👋

Thanks for checking out **PokéFollower** - a browser extension that brings a little nostalgic joy to your browsing experience with cute retro 2D Pokémon sprites that follow your cursor around the web.

---

### About This Project

PokéFollower started as a personal experiment to recapture that warm, playful feeling of having a companion by your side while you work and browse. Whether you're working on a project, browsing social media, or just need a small friend to keep you company, ***PokéFollower is here to make your screen time a bit more fun.*** It was my girlfriends idea, after I mentioned I wanted to make a plugin.

---

### About Me

My name is **Ali**. I am trying to build more things in my life, and this plugin was one of my first accomplishments in learning how to do so. 

---

### Your Feedback Matters

This extension is still evolving, and your input helps shape what comes next. Whether you've found a bug, have a feature idea, or just want to share your experience, I'd love to hear from you.

### What's Next?

I'm working on adding more Pokémon generations, potentially new behaviors (like attacks or sitting), and potentially emotes. If you have ideas or requests, let me know!

Thanks for being part of this journey. Happy browsing, and may your Pokémon companion bring you good vibes!
